About Verza

At Verza, we believe creators deserve financial tools that are as innovative and dynamic as their work. Founded by a team passionate about empowering the self-employed, we built Verza to tackle the unique challenges creators face, from complex contracts to unpredictable income.

Our mission is to simplify financial management, protect your income, and provide the clarity you need to focus on what you do best: creating. We're starting where the money begins—your deal—and expanding to become your essential financial operating system.
